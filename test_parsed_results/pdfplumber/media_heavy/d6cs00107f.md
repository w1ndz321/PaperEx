5.4. Shortsummary
In this section, we outlined biological strategies that achieve
active control of PTMs by exploiting the high selectivity of
enzymes. Biological systems, including engineered enzymes
and chemical dimerizers, offer programmable and reversible
modulation of PTMs through precise molecular recognition.
With these platforms in hand, their therapeutic potential is
increasingly being explored as novel drug modalities that
access protein higher-order structures and functions via PTM
control. Advancing translational aspects, including ADMET
properties, together with further improvements in reactivity
and selectivity, represents a critical next step toward practical
applications.
